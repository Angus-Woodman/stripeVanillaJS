About

Hello, and thank you for clicking through and having a look at my Online Christmas Show!

This online exhibition aims to show you my shape-shifting practice; taking you from 2019 to 2020.

I have now been working as an artist for a year now. Throughout this journey I have found many different styles and methods to communicate how I think and feel. Although my approach has taken many twists and turns, through reflecting on my practice I have noticed that I continue to explore the same themes; capturing characters, story telling and evoking the sensation of a dream. Over the last year I have developed a language of symbols and personalities; Mr Moon, Fish Eye, Pineapple People, Sailing to The Sun, The Cobble Stone King, The Long River, The Deer, Mr Rose and the Wild Ones. All of these figures are retaliated to stories I have written about my work. Through my writing and art I feel as though I know these characters like the back of my hand, as each of them have become little parts of me and I feel as though I carry them with me wherever I go. I have always struggled with literature, but ironically it is my passion for writing that now laces my work together.

It has been an absolute pleasure waking up every morning and discovering new mediums and means of expression. I honestly can’t thank all of those who have supported me throughout the last year enough. It is your support that has allowed me to get to where I am today and wake up each morning doing what I love.

Thank you and enjoy!
